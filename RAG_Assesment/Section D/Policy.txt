Food Delivery Refund Policy

Our food delivery platform is committed to providing a reliable and smooth delivery service.
If an order arrives more than 30 minutes later than the estimated delivery time,
customers may be eligible for a partial refund or delivery credit.

Delays caused by bad weather, heavy traffic, road closures, or other situations beyond
our control are not eligible for compensation.

If a customer receives the wrong item, the issue must be reported within 24 hours.
The customer should provide a photo of the received item along with the order details.
After verification, a replacement item or a full refund will be provided.

If any item is missing from the order, the customer should contact customer support
within 24 hours. After verification, a full refund will be issued for the missing item.
Delivery charges are normally not refunded unless the missing item significantly
affects the order.

Customers may cancel an order before the restaurant starts preparing it.
In this case, a full refund will be processed within 3 to 5 business days.
If food preparation has already started, only a partial refund may be available,
depending on the preparation status.

Refund requests are usually processed within 5 to 7 business days after approval.
Customers will receive an email confirmation when the refund has been initiated.
The actual refund time depends on the customer's bank or payment provider.

If food arrives damaged or is of poor quality, the customer should report the issue
within 24 hours and provide clear photographs.
Customer support will review the complaint and may approve a partial or full refund.

Customer support is available 24 hours a day through live chat, email, and phone
to help with refunds, cancellations, delivery issues, and payment-related questions.
